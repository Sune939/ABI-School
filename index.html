Our Programs (Academics)

Designed for the learners of today and leaders of tomorrow, our programs reflect the diversity and individuality of our student body. Explore a curriculum that includes:

Preschool and Kindergarten

Our preschool and kindergarten programs focus on early childhood development, fostering a love for learning through play and exploration.

Elementary School

Our elementary school curriculum emphasizes foundational skills in literacy, mathematics, and science, while promoting social and emotional development.

Junior High School

Our junior high school program prepares students for high school with a focus on critical thinking, problem-solving, and effective communication.

High School

Our high school program offers a rigorous curriculum with advanced placement courses, extracurricular activities, and college counseling services.
